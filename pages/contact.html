Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical engagement process?

Our engagement typically begins with an initial consultation to understand your specific needs and challenges. Following this, we'll provide a tailored proposal outlining our recommended approach, timeline, and investment. Once approved, we'll begin the assessment phase, followed by strategy development and implementation support.

How long does a typical consulting project take?

Project duration varies based on the scope and complexity of your needs. Targeted assessments may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive framework implementations typically range from 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our proposal phase.

Do you work with organizations of all sizes?

Yes, we provide consulting services to financial institutions of all sizes, from established banks to emerging fintech startups. Our approach is tailored to your specific needs, scale, and complexity.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in risk management for financial services, including commercial banking, investment management, fintech, payment processing, lending platforms, and insurance. Our expertise is particularly focused on model risk management and regulatory compliance.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

Client confidentiality is paramount in our work. We establish comprehensive non-disclosure agreements at the outset of each engagement and maintain strict data security protocols throughout our collaboration.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support options, including periodic reviews, training sessions, and advisory services. We can discuss these options based on your specific needs and preferences.
