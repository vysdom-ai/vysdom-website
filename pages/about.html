Professional Background

With over 15 years of experience in financial risk management, I provide specialized consulting services to banks and fintech companies seeking to enhance their risk management frameworks and ensure regulatory compliance.

My expertise spans model risk management, regulatory compliance, and the development of comprehensive risk assessment methodologies tailored to the unique challenges faced by financial institutions in today's rapidly evolving landscape.

Having worked with institutions ranging from global banks to innovative fintech startups, I bring a versatile perspective that combines traditional risk management principles with forward-thinking approaches to emerging challenges.
